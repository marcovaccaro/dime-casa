Cookie Policy Generator will advice your visitors of your cookie policy and will generate a cookie policy that will inform them about your cookies.

== Description ==

Cookie Policy Generator is proudly provided by nibirumail.com to solve all your needs about the new EU/Italy Cookie Law.

This plugin will show a black bar (you have to make a visible design break into your layout) to get the explicit consensus from your visitors in saving cookies on your website.

This plugin offers you a customized web page hosted on our website that will show your visitors your custom Cookie Policy based on your website cookies list. 

Cookie Policy Generator will call a remote js file hosted by our server: https://nibirumail.com/docs/scripts/nibirumail.cookie.min.js that will generate all necessary HTML code and ask our web services to generate and mantain up-to-date your Cookie Policy page.

Features:

    You can customize your cookie policy with a custom URL
    You can customize the cookie advice text in your language.
	You will receive a custom Cookie Policy Page under our domain.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `cookie-policy-generator` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. That's it!
